Hingol National Park

Hingol National Park (also spelled Hangul or Hingula) is the largest national park in Pakistan. It is located along the Makran Coastal Highway in the provinces of Balochistan (mainly) and partially Sindh.

Hazarganji–Chiltan National Park

Hazarganji–Chiltan National Park is located near Quetta, Balochistan, Pakistan. It was declared a national park in 1980 to protect the unique wildlife and mountainous ecosystem of the region

Learn More

Lal Suhanra National Park

Lal Suhanra National Park is located near Bahawalpur, Punjab, Pakistan. It was declared a national park in 1972 and is one of the oldest national parks in Pakistan.

Learn More

Deosai National Park

Deosai National Park is one of Pakistan’s most famous protected areas, known as the “Land of Giants” and the second-highest plateau in the world. It is located in Gilgit-Baltistan, between Skardu and Astore.

Learn More

Khunjerab National Park

Khunjerab National Park is Pakistan’s highest-altitude national park and one of the most important protected areas in the country. It is located in Gilgit-Baltistan, near the Pakistan–China border.

Learn More

Central Karakoram National Park (CKNP)

Central Karakoram National Park is the largest national park in Pakistan and one of the most spectacular mountain protected areas in the world. It is located in Gilgit-Baltistan and covers the heart of the Karakoram Range.

Learn More

Margalla Hills National Park

Margalla Hills National Park is located in Islamabad, Pakistan. It is known for its green hills, hiking trails, and wildlife, making it a popular spot for nature lovers and tourists.

Learn More

Ayubia National Park

Ayubia National Park is one of the most well-known and accessible national parks in Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, Pakistan. It is famous for its temperate forests, hiking trails, and rich wildlife.

Learn More

Kirthar National Park

Kirthar National Park is located in Sindh, Pakistan, stretching across the Kirthar Mountain Range between Karachi and the Balochistan border. It was declared a national park in 1974 and is one of the largest national parks in Pakistan.
